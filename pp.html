Palak Paneer

by Kriti Dhar

Spinach-based curry with paneer cubes, seasoned with garlic, ginger, and Indian spices, a nutritious vegetarian staple.

Recipe:-

  • Blanch spinach, blend into a smooth paste.
  • Saut√© onions, ginger, and garlic in oil.
  • Add spinach paste, spices, and cook.
  • In a pan, heat butter, add the paste and cream.
  • Add paneer cubes, simmer until flavors meld.
  • Garnish with cream or butter, serve hot.

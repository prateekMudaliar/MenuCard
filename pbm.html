Paneer Butter Masala

by Kriti Dhar

Creamy tomato-based curry with paneer cubes, butter, and aromatic spices, a popular Indian vegetarian dish.

Recipe:-

  • Saut√© onions, ginger, and garlic in oil.
  • Add tomatoes, cashews, and spices, cook until soft.
  • Blend into a smooth paste, strain if needed.
  • In a pan, heat butter, add the paste, and cook.
  • Add paneer cubes, cream, sugar, and simmer.
  • Garnish with cream and coriander, serve hot.

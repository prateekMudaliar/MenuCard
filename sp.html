Shaahi Paneer

by Kriti Dhar

Royal Indian dish featuring paneer cubes cooked in a rich, creamy gravy made from cashews, cream, and spices.

Recipe:-

  • Saut√© onions, ginger, and garlic in oil.
  • Add cashews, tomatoes, and spices, cook until soft.
  • Blend into a smooth paste.
  • In a pan, heat butter, add the paste and cream.
  • Add paneer cubes, simmer until well-cooked.
  • Garnish with cream and chopped nuts.
